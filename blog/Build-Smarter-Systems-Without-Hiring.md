2. Build Smarter Systems Without Hiring

Why would you care? Because hiring help is expensive, and most small teams need leverage before they need more people.

This blog can sell evolutionary algorithms as a way to improve your business systems over time. You can frame it like this: instead of manually guessing which prompt, process, or agent setup works best, let the system test, compare, and improve. That makes it feel like a hidden optimization layer for a lean business.

Angle: “How evolutionary algorithms help small businesses find better workflows without adding staff.”
