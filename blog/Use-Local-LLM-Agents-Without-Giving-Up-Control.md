3. Use Local LLM Agents Without Giving Up Control

Why would you care? Because a lot of freelancers and small businesses want AI benefits without sending everything to a third-party platform.

This blog can focus on local LLM agents as a privacy-friendly, cost-controlled option. The pitch is that you can run smart assistants on your own setup, keep sensitive info closer to home, and still automate support, research, content, or admin work. That makes it attractive to service businesses that care about trust and control.
